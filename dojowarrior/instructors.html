    Our Instructors

    Sensei Anthony Carrano

    Trained in the Hoteikan System since the age of 3. Currently a 3rd degree black belt. Ranked in U.S.J.A (United States Judo Association) as a Nikkyu. Competed & placed in numerous competitions, ranging from AAU Karate Nationals Competition, Judo Collegiate Nationals, Kickboxing (Full Contact) at the Capitale, Extreme Team MMA etc.... Certified as a Physical Education teacher and coach in NYC. Certified Strength & Conditioning Specialist CSCS.

    Sensei Dan Notwicz

    Sensei Dan was born and raised in Sunset Park Brooklyn. Starting Martial Arts at the Hoteikan dojo after it was recommended by friend, he has over a decade worth of experience in this system.

    Sensei Dan has been teaching children's classes since earning his first degree and is currently working towards his second degree. Outside of the dojo, Dan works as a project manager for a lighting company and enjoys working out to offset the desk job. Other interests include playing guitar and basketball and traveling.

    Sensei Jean Calderone

    Jean has trained with the Hoteikan Dojo since the age of 5. With over 15 years of experience in the Hoteikan system, she considers the dojo staff, teachers, and classmates like a second family. Currently a first degree black belt, she is a teacher's assistant for the Lil' Dragons class. Outside of training, she is a freelance comic artist and illustrator, and has contributed a number of art works for the Hoteikan Dojo.

    Sensei Rebecca Carrano

    (Lil'Dragons/Cardio Kickboxing) - Rebecca is a second degree Black Belt and is the first female adult to receive a Black Belt with the Hoteikan System. She has taken seminars with Tae Boe's famous Billy Blanks, Kickboxing Champion Kathy Long and Super Foot Bill Wallace. Rebecca's Cardio Kickboxing classes are intense but rewarding. She has been a Kickboxing instructor for over 10 years and has trained in the martial arts for 13 years. She is also a certified coach and has assisted coaching Hoteikan Dojo's tournament team. She enjoys training hard and effectively. She teaches private lessons, Lil'Dragons and adult Kickboxing classes . She holds a Masters Degree in Early Childhood Education and has utilized her expertise in childhood development to construct a Lil'Dragon's Pre-Karate program, specifically for children ages 3-5 years old. She has currently implemented a book reading program, which is held once a month, connecting literacy and character development to karate training. Rebecca is a licensed Early Childhood Educator, and is currently teaching in a NYC public school. Not only is Rebecca a mother, and a wife, she has also fought the greatest fight a woman could fight, Breast Cancer.

    Sensei Roxanne Carrano

    Sensei Roxanne has trained in the Hoteikan Dojo since the age of 3. She was the very first female Junior Black Belt of the Hoteikan System. She has competed, coached and judged for AAU Karate championships. At age 9, Roxanne placed first place in the Junior World Championships for karate. Roxanne has her Bachelor's Degree in Graphic Design and has done a lot of the print work for the Dojo. Roxanne also has a passion for dance and has studied ballet, tap, jazz, modern, African Caribbean, hip-hop and some salsa, merengue and bachata. She currently is licensed in Zumba Fitness , Zumbatomic (-zumba for kids) and Zumba Toning.

    Sensei Tom Carrano

    He's an 8th degree blackbelt who began training in the arts at age 11. Shihan Carrano's been teaching the martial arts for over 25 years. He's Hoteikan Dojo's chief instructor in Bayridge Brooklyn and oversees all classes, teaches the Black belt class and the instructors/leadership program.

    Well versed in Karate and Jujitsu, Shihan Carrano is well known and respected by his peers in the martial arts. His love and expertise in the martial arts is evident in his teaching. He teaches all ages and levels and conducts learning in a strict but fun and creative environment. He has instructed and trained winners of Regional, National, and World Championship levels. Not only does Shihan Carrano train students; he himself trains for tournaments and has won many competitions with his flawless Katas and crafty sparring techniques. He's actively involved in the community and volunteers his time helping students in public schools with a bully buster program. He's a licensed Massage Therapist and specializes in Shiatsu. A mechanic by trade where he's applied his skills for over 25 years with the NYC Transit Authority and is presently chairman of the largest transit facility in North America. Shihan Carrano is also the founder of the X-treme Team Martial Arts league. Graduate of Cornell's leadership program, a husband, father & grandfather.
