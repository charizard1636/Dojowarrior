    How does the ranking system work?

    Kids & Adults (8yrs to 100 yrs old)

    Rank
    8 Hachi Kyu
    7 Shichi Kyu
    6 Rok Kyu
    5 Go Kyu
    4 Yon Kyu
    3 San Kyu
    2 Ni Kyu
    1 Ik Kyu
    Level Belt Color
    Beginners White, Yellow stripe
    Novice Yellow, Orange
    Intermediate Green, Purple
    Advance Brown, Black Brown

    Belt KYU
    Black Belt Sho dan - excellence of basics
    Ni dan - excellence of application
    San dan - the "fighting" rank
    Black w/Red & White Yon dan - master/teacher
    Go dan - master/teacher
    Black w/Red & White Rokyu dan - Headmaster
    Shichi dan - Headmaster
    Hachi dan - Headmaster
    Red Kyu dan - Grandmaster
    Ju dan - Grandmaster

    Lil' Dragon Rank System

    Each Little Dragon promotion is 1/3 of the material required for the Adult ranking system.


    Rank Belt Color
    10 White
    9 White w/Yellow stripe
    8 White w/Orange stripe
    7 White w/Blue stripe
    6 White w/Green stripe


    Upon reaching the age of 8 , all Lil' Dragons graduate to the Kids class. Their next promotion will put them in the appropriate Kids/Adults belt rank.
