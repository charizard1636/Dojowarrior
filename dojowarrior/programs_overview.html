    The Hoteikan System

    The Hoteikan System seeks to establish perfection of character, spiritual and physical well being, and dedication to the Martial Arts. These principles are reflected in the Hoteikan Dojo Kun. The System has three major components: Jujitsu, Karate, and Judo. These are taught individually, or in combination with the parent Hoteikan Martial Art System
    In addition to these major Arts, the Hoteikan System has taken the very best of many other Arts to form a complete System with self preservation and the perfection of character in mind. To this goal our members from around the nation meet 2 times per year to share these ideals and continue our journey through the Martial Arts, under the tutelage of Shodai Soke Emilio Claudio and Soke Santiago Armstrong.
    We currently have thousands of students and members around the world, both in the Military protecting our country and in the private sector. In addition, we have member schools in: New York, Arizona, Georgia, and South Dakota & New Jersey.
    Our Mission is simple, The Hoteikan System is a complete Martial Arts system, come train with us and spread the word! www.hoteikan.com .


    What is Karate?

    Karate, literally meaning "Empty Hand" is a system of unarmed defense. It is based on discipline of the mind and body.

    It includes training to develop balance which enhances your understanding of leverage and generation of power. It involved blocking, striking, kicking and countering all manner of attack.


    JUDO

    Judo is the sport of jujitsu. During a time of change in Japan a man named Jigoro Kano wanted to preserve the timeless qualities of traditional jujitsu – loyalty, discipline, resolve, honor, morality – and discard the qualities in which the martial artist learns techniques to hurt, maim and kill. To do this, Kano made judo a safe, rewarding and challenging sport for the evolution of Jujitsu. Judo consists of rolls, falls, joint locks, grappling, throws, and take downs as a safer way to compete with your Jujitsu techniques.


    What is Jujitsu?

    Jujitsu literally meaning "The Gentle Art" is an ancient form of self-defense based on using the natural flow of an attacker against themselves. It includes joint locks, grappling, arresting and striking with the least amount of effort through maximum efficiency.


    What is Kardio-Kickboxing?

    This fitness craze has rocked the nation! Now America's favorite way to shape up and rated #1 calorie burning workout while learning self defense skills.

    Kickboxing combines the hands of western boxing and the kicks of eastern karate.

    Children's Program

    At the Hoteikan Dojo, our children's programs are second to none. We realize that although the ability to defend oneself is important, there are other benefits children can receive from being involved in the Martial Arts. This is why our unique program of work and fun is designed to instill:

    ● Effort and Concentration

    ● Character and Self Esteem

    ● Sincerity and Respect

    ● Etiquette

    By embodying the above principles into our children's self defense classes, the Hoteikan Dojo has made a strong commitment to the development of today's youth. Children can start with our Pre-K karate program for ages 3 through 5. This class prepares them for our Lil'Dragons class, ages 5 through 7. Children can then enter our kid's class, ages 8 through teen, where they learn to think memorize, and concentrate in a fun way. Our special structured programs will improve your child's performance in all other sports as well as improving study habits and concentration.

    Zumba

    Zumba Fitness® is the only Latin-inspired dance-fitness program that blends red-hot international music, created by Grammy Award-winning producers, and contagious steps to form a "fitness-party" that is downright addictive. Since its inception in 2001, the Zumba program has grown to become the world's largest – and most successful – dance-fitness program with more than 14 million people of all shapes, sizes and ages taking weekly Zumba classes in over 140,000 locations across more than 150 countries.


    Zumbatomic

    Designed exclusively for kids (ages 4-12), Zumbatomic classes are rockin’, high-energy fitness-parties packed with specially choreographed, kid-friendly routines and all the music kids love, like hip-hop, reggaeton, cumbia and more. Parents love Zumbatomic because of the effects it has on kids, increasing their focus and self-confidence, boosting metabolism and enhancing coordination. Zumbatomic rounds out family fitness goals by giving parents a great reason to head to the gym – for themselves, for their kids, for a totally fun family experience.
